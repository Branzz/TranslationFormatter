## Meditation on Feeling of Peacefulness

etaṁ this is
santaṁ peaceful
etaṁ this is
paṇītaṁ sublime
yadidaṁ which is
sabbasaṅkhārasamatho calming of all mental conditioning
sabbūpadhipaṭinissaggo not giving forth all objects of attachment
taṇhākkhayo ending of desire
virāgo dispassion
nirodho cessation of perception and feeling
nibbānan cessation (happiness)
